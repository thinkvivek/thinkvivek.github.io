About Us

The Sanrakshnarth Sanstha is committed to improving the lives of India's underprivileged with a special focus on children, women and youth.

We follow the principles of transparency, secularism, and accountability in all of our work activities.

Objectives
  • Promotion of preventive health programs.
  • To make arrangements for free health check-ups/treatment for needy people.
  • To provide assistance in the care, establishment, support, operation, maintenance etc. of monuments of national cultural, religious, spiritual importance of any nation, culture, religion, sect etc.
  • To build and support animal shelters, Gaushalas, Panjrapaul and life-giving institutions for the service and care of animals and birds.
  • To give direct financial assistance to farmers or to provide assistance in establishment, support, operation, maintenance etc. of institutions, organizations, societies for the progress and upliftment of farmers, agriculture and nature.
  • To organize public meetings, seminars, general rallies, awareness programmes, competitions/competitions etc. to achieve the objectives of the organization.
  • To develop / maintain charitable institutions like hospitals, medical colleges, publicity institutes, dispensaries, child welfare centers and / or such established in Rajasthan and to provide then grants in cash or kind.
  • Establishment, operation, support, maintenance etc. of schools, colleges, libraries, reading rooms, universities, laboratories and provide help in the field of education.
  • To establish, administer, maintain, and assist scholarship students, and assist them with distribution of books, stipends, medals or other incentives for study, without discrimination of any color, race, sex, caste or religion.
  • To promote science, literature, music, theatrical science, upliftment of fine arts, conservation of historical monuments in Rajasthan.
  • To establish, maintain, operate, or assist old age homes, orphanages, rehabilitation centers and to help needy people, orphan children, widows and old people.
  • To provide development, maintenance, education, food, clothing or any other kind of assistance to physically handicapped / disabled or mentally handicapped persons.
  • To provide aid and relief to the people suffering from natural calamities like famine, storm (cyclone), earthquake, flood, arson, epidemic etc.
  • To work for the promotion, protection, and upliftment of needy and helpless communities / families.
  • Organizing meetings / seminars / general rallies / public meetings and awareness programs etc. to create social awareness for promoting water conservation.
