About Us

The Sanrakshnarth Sanstha is committed to improving the lives of India's underprivileged with a special focus on children, women and youth.

We follow the principles of transparency, secularism, and accountability in all of our work activities.

Objectives
  • Promotion of preventive health programs.
  • To make arrangements for health check-up and health check-up of the needy persons, their treatment etc., free of cost.
  • To provide assistance in the care, establishment, support, operation, maintenance etc. of monuments of national cultural, religious, spiritual importance of any nation, culture, religion, sect etc.
  • To provide support in the establishment, support, operation, maintenance etc. of animal shelters, gaushalas, Panjrapaul, life-giving institutions for the service and care of animals and birds.
  • To give direct financial assistance to farmers or to provide assistance in establishment, support, operation, maintenance etc. of institutions, organizations, societies for the progress and upliftment of farmers, agriculture and nature.
  • To organize meetings/meetings, seminars, general rallies, public meetings, awareness programmes, competitions/competitions etc. to achieve the objectives of the organization.
  • To develop / maintain such charitable institutions like hospitals, medical colleges, publicity institutes, dispensaries, child welfare centers and / or such established in Rajasthan for the use and benefit of Aajman and to give grant in cash or kind.
  • Establishment, operation, support, maintenance etc. of schools, colleges, libraries, reading rooms, universities, laboratories, research in the field of education and other institutions for the use of students/staff as well as for the spread of knowledge and growth and development of education among the general public. To provide assistance in the form of a grant or in kind.
  • To establish, administer, maintain and otherwise assist students of scholarships, including the distribution of books, stipends, medals or other incentives for study, without discrimination of any colour, race, sex, caste or religion.
  • In Rajasthan, to promote science, literature, music, theatrical science, upliftment of fine arts, conservation of historical monuments, research and other institutions working for such purposes, their support, operation, maintenance, grants or assistance in kind. promote them by providing.
  • To establish, maintain, operate or assist old age homes, orphanages or to assist or make arrangements for the help and rehabilitation of poor, needy and helpless people, orphan children, widows and old people.
  • To provide for the development, maintenance, education, food, clothing or any other kind of assistance to physically handicapped and disabled or mentally handicapped persons.
  • To provide assistance and relief to the suffering and needy people during natural calamities like famine, storm (cyclone), earthquake, flood, arson, epidemic etc.
  • To work for the promotion, protection and upliftment of the people of the needy and helpless community, family.
  • Organizing meeting / meeting / seminar / general rally / public meeting / awareness programs etc. to create social awareness for water conservation, promotion and water conservation.
